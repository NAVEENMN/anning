Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

Overview

Anning is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data. All data entered into the app is stored locally on your device unless explicitly stated otherwise.

By using Anning, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

⸻

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect personal information such as:
    •    Name
    •    Email address
    •    Phone number
    •    Location data
    •    Contacts
    •    Identifiers tied to your identity

⸻

User-Provided Content

Anning allows you to enter and manage:
    •    Research paper links or metadata
    •    Notes and research objectives
    •    Organizational metadata created by you

This content:
    •    Is stored locally on your device
    •    Is not transmitted to our servers
    •    Is not accessed by the developer

⸻

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally using on-device storage mechanisms provided by the operating system.

We do not operate backend servers that store your app data.

⸻

Third-Party Services

Anning does not:
    •    Use third-party analytics tools
    •    Use advertising SDKs
    •    Track user behavior
    •    Share data with external services

If future versions introduce optional online features, this policy will be updated accordingly.

⸻

Data Sharing

We do not share data with:
    •    Advertisers
    •    Analytics providers
    •    Data brokers
    •    Third-party organizations

⸻

Data Security

Because data is stored locally on your device:
    •    Security depends on your device’s operating system protections
    •    The app does not transmit sensitive data over the network

⸻

Children’s Privacy

Anning does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.
